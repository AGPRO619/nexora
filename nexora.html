// About

What is Nexora?

Nexora is a free browser arcade with a sci-fi twist. Pick a game, press play, and you're in. No sign-ups. No downloads. No clutter.

Every game streams live from our open library on the cloud, so the collection keeps growing without you ever needing to refresh.

Built for speed. Designed for fun. Made for everyone.
